## FAQ

### Which countries are covered by Carindex?
Carindex currently covers 20+ countries in Europe, North America, and South America. The complete list is available in the "Data & Coverage" section. We regularly add new countries based on our clients' demand.

### What is the accuracy of the market prices provided?
Accuracy depends on the number of available comparables. For popular models with more than 50 similar listings, our accuracy exceeds 95%. Each market price is accompanied by a confidence index (0-100%) that reflects the reliability of the estimate.

### How frequently are data updated?
Price and inventory data are updated several times per day. Trend analyses are recalculated monthly with a 30-month history. Alerts are triggered in real-time as soon as an event matching your criteria is detected.

### Is the API available on all plans?
The API is exclusively available on the Performance plan. It allows complete integration with your CRM, DMS, or internal tools. For Start and Comfort plans, access is via web interface only.

### Is there a commitment period?
No, all our plans are commitment-free. You can cancel at any time. The 14-day free trial allows you to test all features of your chosen plan before committing.

### Can I change plans during my subscription?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is adjusted pro-rata.

### Are data exportable?
Yes, you can export your searches, analyses, and reports in CSV or Excel format. The API (Performance plan) also allows automated data export.

### Does Carindex work on mobile?
Yes, our web interface is fully responsive and optimized for tablets and smartphones. You can access your data, create alerts, and view your analyses from any device.

### What is the difference between "Market Price" and "Quote"?
"Market Price" is an estimate based on analysis of similar listings. "Quote" additionally includes analysis of model attractiveness, average sales time, and positioning recommendations. Available on Comfort and Performance plans.

### How does Carindex guarantee data reliability?
We use advanced algorithms to filter fraudulent listings, duplicates, and errors. Each data point is validated before integration. Our main sources are AutoScout24, mobile.de, LeBonCoin, and other major platforms, scraped via robust and compliant methods.
